IV · Bash Manager · An AI Team On Shift
An AI Team · On Shift

Not a tool. A team of AI agents - at work.

Bash Manager isn't a dashboard. Four AI agents work the venue tonight - analyst, moderator, host, operator. They write the brief, queue the fixes, run hospitality on the floor, and operate the place from one chat. The manager just decides.

01 The Brief - Analytics Bash AI's read on the venue's performance - revenue, cohorts, retention, conversion. Analytics with a verdict, not just charts.
02 Needs You - Actions that age Nonstop checks for expired posters, broken menus, negative reviews, idle stations. A tight fix-list before each one stops mattering.
03 Live Hospitality - Engagement Profile-aware live actions - recognize, greet, seat, save, upsell. The right move for this guest, in the moment.
04 Bash Agent - Operations No conventional dashboard. Ask anything in chat - re-price a table, draft an event, build a guest list. Done in seconds.

VII · Bash · The Consumer App
The Consumer App · First Look

One app for all your outings.

Bash is the app for going out - squads, check-ins, backfill, Wrapped, all in one place. Plan with your crew inside the app, walk in and get recognized, and keep track of the journey of your outings. Bash AI sits inside it - surfacing the best outings with direct bookable links. No long chats.

01 Squads - Inside Bash Squads live under the Bash app - not WhatsApp, not group chats. Plan with your crew inside Bash, and Bash AI surfaces the best outings for the squad's vibe + budget, with direct bookable links.
02 Check-in - Recognized at the door Arrive and get spotted - perks unlock, the table is held, the cover is comped. The venue knows you're here before you've finished saying hi.
03 Backfill - Profile, day one Sign up and Bash backfills your profile from your past nights out - venues, squads, taste, streaks. You don't start from zero; the app already knows who you go out with and what you go out for.
04 Wrapped - Your year out The nights, the venues, the squad, the streaks - your social year in review. Shareable, screenshot-ready, and the reason next year's plans get bigger.
